CEO Profile

Jomarie C. Bajar

Age: 20
Nationality: Filipino
School: Clark College of Science and Technology
Course: Bachelor of Science in Office Administration
Position: CEO & Creative Director, Maison d'Élan

Background:

Born and raised in the small province of Masbate, Jomarie Bajar grew up in a broken home, raised by his mother Mary Ann Cos, a seamstress who taught him resilience and creativity. Life was tough, but it sparked his passion for design—especially elegant footwear. In 2005, his mother started a small sandal-making business to support them. Years later, Jomarie revived it as Maison d'Élan, a luxury heels brand that blends Filipino craftsmanship with Parisian flair. What started as a humble home project now serves clients across the Philippines and beyond. While studying at Clark College of Science and Technology, Jomarie balances academics with leading one of the most talked-about young fashion brands in the country.
