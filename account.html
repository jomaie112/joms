New Headquarter

In 2024, Maison d'Élan unveiled its first-ever global headquarters in the heart of Bonifacio Global City, the Philippines' premier business and lifestyle district. A bold symbol of modern Filipino luxury, the headquarters reflects the brand’s core philosophy: elegance rooted in resilience. The five-story glass-and-concrete building features a state-of-the-art design studio, artisan workshop, runway showroom, and VIP fitting lounges inspired by Parisian couture houses. The space also includes an eco-conscious Materials Innovation Lab, where local artisans collaborate on sustainable leather alternatives and handcrafted detailing. Heart Evangelista become a local ambassador

Paris, France — Maison d’Élan, the Paris-based luxury heels collection renowned for its sculptural silhouettes and artisanal design, proudly announces Heart Evangelista as its newest global brand ambassador. A celebrated style icon, artist, and fashion muse, Heart Evangelista embodies the very essence of Maison d’Élan: modern grace, unapologetic elegance, and global sophistication. Her presence on international red carpets—from Paris to Milan, New York to Manila—has made her a perennial fixture in the world’s most prestigious fashion circles. With her discerning eye for detail and fearless approach to personal style, Evangelista is the natural choice to represent Élan’s vision of empowered femininity.

Kim Kardashian as the New Global ambassador

Kim Kardashian is now the official global ambassadress of Maison d'Élan, a luxury heels brand founded by Filipino designer Jomarie Bajar. The partnership was signed in 2025 and marks a major milestone for the brand, which started in a small province in the Philippines. Kim will represent Maison d'Élan in international campaigns, wearing exclusive designs from the upcoming collection, including the signature “Divina” heels. She chose to work with the brand because of its powerful story, elegant craftsmanship, and commitment to empowering women through fashion. This collaboration brings Maison d'Élan into the global spotlight, showing the world how far Filipino creativity and talent can go.
