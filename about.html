About Us

Company Summary

Founded in 2005 by Jomarie Bajar Maison d'Élan was established with a bold and visionary purpose: to become the premier destination for luxury high-heeled footwear. Born from a passion for elegance, femininity, and timeless design, the company was created to unite the world’s most iconic fashion houses under one sophisticated roof. Today, Maison d'Élan is known globally as the definitive source for the most exquisite heels from the likes of Prada, Louis Vuitton, Chanel, and Yves Saint Laurent. What began as a curated boutique has since blossomed into a global fashion authority, beloved by celebrities, stylists, and luxury aficionados alike. Our mission from day one has been clear: to celebrate the artistry of footwear and empower our clientele through style, confidence, and sophistication. Headquartered in Philippines, our flagship showroom sets the tone for our brand: an immersive blend of luxury, exclusivity, and refined aesthetics. Designed for those who understand that heels are more than accessories — they are declarations of identity Maison d'Élan offers collections that reflect both heritage and haute innovation. From sleek patent stilettos to sculptural runway pieces, each heel tells a story of meticulous craftsmanship and design excellence.
